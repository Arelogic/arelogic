eSports Team "Logic Gaming" Logo Design

Logic Gaming is a new eSports team for Overwatch and asked for a logo that suits the best to the team.

Logic Design used image of a rook from chess and a speech balloon which contains the name of the team in order to make it look like both L and G which stands for Logic Gaming.

  • Client: Logic Gaming
  • Date: September 2017
  • Service: eSports Team
